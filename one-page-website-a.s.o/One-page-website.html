About the City

Queretaro city is located in central Mexico, capital of the state of Queretaro.
Founded in 1531, it is a historical place that has preserved its very characteristic spanish colonial architecture, making it an amzing place to visit and to have fun, as it has also grown to become the biggest city of the state. There multiple places for families to visit and also has a very active nightlife, something very unique as you will encounter unlimited options for things to do at any time and for everyone.
It is also surrounded of many small towns like San Miguel de Allende, Tequisquiapan, Bernal, and others, at 40 min drive each; where you can also find very unique and historical places, and lots of things to do, lots of places to visit and delicious traditional food to try, all in the nice usual warm weather of the area.
